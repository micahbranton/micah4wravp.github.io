About the Candidate
"Be a BROTHER,vote for Branton."

Micah Branton is a dedicated leader, servant, and brother who has committed himself to uplifting the Western Region through intentional action and unwavering brotherhood.

His campaign is anchored in one word: BROTHER — an acronym that captures his full vision for the region: Brotherhood, Regional Engagement, Opportunity, Thriving Financially, Honoring the Legacy, Exposure, and Reaching Through Service.

Micah believes real leadership means showing up, connecting members, and building structures that give every brother in the Western Region the resources and visibility they need to succeed.

The Platform

What BROTHER Stands For

BROTHER
Brotherhood  ·  Regional Engagement  ·  Opportunity  ·  Thrive Financially
Honor the Legacy  ·  Exposure  ·  Reach Through Service
B
Brotherhood

Connect brothers across all generations and levels of engagement. Reclaim inactive brothers through intentional outreach and reintegration. Strengthen retention through meaningful connection and involvement.

R
Regional Engagement

Coordinate collaboration between the region and chapters. Organize regional events that unify members in person and virtually. Build consistent participation across geographic areas.

O
Opportunity

Create career, leadership, and business opportunities for brothers and chapters. Connect chapters to partnerships, collaborations, and external resources. Turn internal networks into pathways for growth and advancement.

T
Thrive Financially

Generate revenue streams to directly support chapter operations. Promote partnerships, sponsorships, and fundraising initiatives. Equip chapters with tools to sustain and grow financially.

H
Honor the Legacy

Preserve traditions and historical identity of the brotherhood. Recognize past contributions and leadership impact. Reinforce values that guide future generations.

E
Exposure

Showcase member achievements and ventures. Increase visibility of chapters, events, and initiatives. Create platforms for skills, talent, and leadership recognition.

R
Reach Through Service

Organize coordinated regional service initiatives. Establish signature service events at the regional level. Strengthen community relationships through consistent engagement.
